## Priority Scoring

**Priority Score = (Hiring Scale × 2) + (R1 Search Overlap × 3) + (Recurring Event × 1)**

- **Hiring Scale:** 1-3 (1=1000-5000, 2=5000-10000, 3=10000+)
- **R1 Search Overlap:** 1-3 (1=low, 2=medium, 3=high volume in R1)
- **Recurring Event:** 0-1 (1=has predictable annual cycle)

## Key Insights

### Market Trends (2025-2026)

1. **AI/ML Hiring Surge:** 53% of US tech job postings now require AI/ML skills (up from 29% in 2024)
2. **India Expansion:** Big Tech added 32,000+ jobs in India in 2025, expected to grow 16-20% in 2026
3. **Entry-Level Decline:** Entry-level tech positions down 73% YoY; focus on specialized roles
4. **Consulting Resilience:** MBB and Big 4 maintaining strong recruiting pipelines
5. **Defense Growth:** Increased federal spending driving aerospace/defense hiring

### Recommendations for Content Prioritization

1. **Immediate Focus:** Google, Amazon, Meta SWE (highest search + active hiring)
2. **Fall 2026 Push:** McKinsey, BCG, Bain, Goldman Sachs (recruiting cycle alignment)
3. **Niche Opportunity:** AI companies (OpenAI, Anthropic) - lower volume but high intent
4. **Emerging:** Defense contractors for cleared professionals
