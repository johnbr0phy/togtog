## Preparation Tips

1. **Technical Depth**: Prepare more than for other PM roles - Google expects technical fluency
2. **Product Design Framework**: Have a structured approach (user, problem, solution, metrics)
3. **Estimation Practice**: Get comfortable with market sizing and Fermi problems
4. **Google Products**: Know Google's product suite well - you'll likely discuss them
5. **STAR Stories**: Prepare for behavioral using STAR method
6. **Writing Skills**: (APM) Practice concise, clear product writing
