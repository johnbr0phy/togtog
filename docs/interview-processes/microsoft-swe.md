### The As-Appropriate (AA) Round
Microsoft's unique final interview:
- Named "As-Appropriate" because content varies based on gaps
- Conducted by senior leader or hiring manager
- Being invited means you're a strong candidate
- Can cover any remaining concerns
- Not everyone gets this round
