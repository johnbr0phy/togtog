## Unique Elements

### Googleyness
Google specifically assesses "Googleyness" - a combination of:
- **Purpose-driven mindset** - Eager to make an impact
- **Intellectual humility** - Open to being wrong
- **Collaborative nature** - Works well in teams
- **Comfort with ambiguity** - Can navigate uncertainty
- **Bias toward action** - Gets things done

**Confidence:** âœ… (Well-documented in official Google materials)

### Hiring Committee Process
Unlike most companies where interviewers make hiring decisions, Google uses an independent hiring committee that:
- Reviews all interview feedback
- Makes the hire/no-hire decision
- Ensures consistency and reduces bias
- Is not influenced by specific team needs

**Confidence:** âœ… (Verified by multiple sources and official process)

### 2025-2026 Updates
- In-person interviews returning to combat AI tool usage during remote interviews
- Increased scrutiny on solution quality
- Tougher algorithmic challenges reported

## Preparation Tips

1. **Coding**: Practice LeetCode medium-hard, focus on explaining thought process
2. **System Design**: Study distributed systems, Google-scale problems
3. **Googleyness**: Prepare STAR-format behavioral stories
4. **Communication**: Practice coding in plain text editors without autocomplete
