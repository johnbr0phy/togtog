> "At Apple, each interview loop is specific to the needs of individual hiring managers â€“ there is no canonical 'Apple interview', and everything is 'made to order' for each specific team."

- Two people interview you simultaneously
- Different perspectives in same session
- Can feel more intense
- More comprehensive evaluation

## Preparation Tips

1. **Research Your Team**: Understand the specific team's domain deeply
2. **Language Choice**: Know what languages the team uses
3. **Apple Ecosystem**: Understand how Apple products/services integrate
4. **Privacy Focus**: Be ready to discuss privacy-conscious design
5. **Excellence Mindset**: Apple values attention to detail
6. **2-Person Dynamics**: Practice with two people asking questions
7. **Practical Problems**: Focus on practical over purely academic challenges
8. **Project Discussion**: Be ready to discuss past projects in depth

## Common Mistakes

- Treating it like a standard FAANG interview (it's not standardized)
- Not researching the specific team
- Ignoring privacy/security considerations in design
- Not being prepared for early termination possibility
- Using the wrong language for the team
