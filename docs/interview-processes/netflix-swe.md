## Preparation Tips

1. **Netflix Culture Deck**: Read Netflix's culture memo thoroughly
2. **Real-World Problems**: Practice problems with ambiguous requirements
3. **System Design**: Focus on streaming, scalability, availability
4. **Culture Stories**: Prepare stories demonstrating each of the 9 values
5. **Product Thinking**: Show you care about user/product impact
6. **Concurrency**: Often comes up in coding questions
7. **Trade-offs**: Be ready to discuss engineering trade-offs, not just optimal solutions

## Common Mistakes

- Treating behavioral as secondary to technical
