## Preparation Tips

1. **Product Sense Framework**: Have a structured approach
   - Clarify goals/constraints
   - Identify users
   - Define problems
   - Propose solutions
   - Discuss metrics

2. **Execution Practice**: Get comfortable with metrics
   - Know how to set goals
   - Understand A/B testing
   - Practice prioritization frameworks

3. **STAR Stories**: Prepare for Leadership & Drive
   - 5-7 strong behavioral stories
   - Cover conflict, influence, resilience
   - Include quantifiable outcomes

4. **Meta Products**: Know Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp deeply
   - Use them regularly
   - Understand business models
   - Think about improvements

5. **Current Trends**: Be aware of Meta's direction
   - AI integration
   - AR/VR (Meta Quest)
   - Creator economy
   - Metaverse vision
